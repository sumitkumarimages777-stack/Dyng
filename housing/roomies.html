FAQs

Questions about Roomies

How does Roomies match me with a roommate near DU?

You create a short profile — your college, campus side, budget, food preference (veg/non-veg), sleep schedule and habits. Roomies shows you compatible DU students looking to share around the same areas and budget, and you connect directly in the app.

Is Roomies safe? Are profiles verified?

Yes. Every Roomies profile is a verified student — we confirm college identity before a profile goes live. No brokers, no random listings, students only.

Can I find a roommate and a PG together on Dyng?

That's the whole point. Match with a compatible roommate on Roomies, then browse Dyng's verified PGs with transparent rent breakdowns and book a double or triple sharing room together — zero brokerage.

Is Roomies free?

Yes, Roomies is completely free for students.
