FAQs

Questions about Dyng

What is Dyng?

Dyng is a super app built for Delhi University students. It brings together verified PG booking and roommate matching, college events and society recruitments, and campus ride booking — all in one platform.

Which features are live right now?

The Housing module is live. You can browse verified PGs near DU North and South Campus with transparent rent breakdowns and zero brokerage, and use Roomies to find a compatible roommate. Events and Rides are launching soon.

How do I find a roommate on Dyng?

Roomies, part of the Housing module, matches you with fellow DU students based on college, budget, food preference and lifestyle — so you can split a PG or flat with someone compatible.

Is Dyng free for students?

Yes. Browsing PGs, matching with roommates and discovering events is free for students, and PG booking has zero brokerage.
